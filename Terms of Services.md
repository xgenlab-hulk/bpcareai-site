## 2. App Description

BPCare AI is a health monitoring application that provides:

- **Heart Rate Monitoring**: Using your device’s camera and flash to detect pulse through photoplethysmography (PPG)
- **Heart Rate Variability (HRV) Analysis**: Advanced algorithms analyze heart rhythm patterns
- **Blood Pressure Recording**: Manual entry and tracking of blood pressure measurements
- **Blood Sugar Monitoring**: Manual entry and tracking of blood glucose levels
- **Health Trend Analysis**: Visual charts and insights based on your recorded data
- **Personalized Health Insights**: Educational information and general wellness suggestions

All analysis and data processing occurs locally on your device using built-in algorithms.
