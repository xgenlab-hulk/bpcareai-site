## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Health Data

When you use BPCare AI, the following information is collected and processed locally on your device:

- **Heart Rate Measurements**: Pulse rate data captured through your device’s camera
- **Heart Rate Variability (HRV)**: Analysis of heart rhythm patterns and variations
- **Blood Pressure Readings**: Manually entered systolic and diastolic pressure values
- **Blood Sugar Levels**: Manually entered glucose measurements
- **Blood Oxygen Estimates**: Calculated values based on heart rate analysis
- **Measurement Timestamps**: Date and time of each health measurement
- **Health Trends**: Calculated averages, patterns, and insights based on your data

### 2.2 Technical Information

- **Device Information**: Device model and iOS version (for app compatibility)
- **App Usage Data**: Features used and measurement frequency (stored locally)
- **Settings Preferences**: Your chosen units, notification settings, and app configurations

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, address, phone number)
- Location data
- Contacts or other device data unrelated to app functionality
- Third-party app data
